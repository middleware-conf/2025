Transportation from City to Vanderbilt University

Vanderbilt University is located in the Midtown area of Nashville, Tennessee, approximately 1.5 miles (2.4 km) southwest of downtown Nashville.

By Car

If you are driving from downtown Nashville, take Broadway (US-70) westbound and turn left onto 21st Avenue South. The campus will be on your right.

By Public Transit

Nashville's public transit system, operated by the Metropolitan Transit Authority (MTA), offers bus services that connect various parts of the city. You can take bus routes that stop near Vanderbilt University. Check the MTA website for route information and schedules.

By Taxi or Rideshare

Taxis and rideshare services like Uber and Lyft are widely available in Nashville. You can easily book a ride to Vanderbilt University from anywhere in the city.

From the Airport

If you are arriving at Nashville International Airport (BNA), you can take a taxi, rideshare, or shuttle service to Vanderbilt University. The airport is located about 8 miles (13 km) east of the campus.

Parking

Vanderbilt University has several parking options available for visitors. Be sure to check the university's parking website for information on parking permits, fees, and designated visitor parking areas.
